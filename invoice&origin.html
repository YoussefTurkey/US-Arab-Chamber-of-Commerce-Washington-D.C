Invoice And Certificate Of Origin Certification

Affixing The Stamp Of The Chamber, And Obtaining Legalization From The An Embassy

The main function of USACC is to provide certification of shipping documents for U.S. businesses exporting goods to the Middle East.

Many companies require the embassy seal after obtaining the chamber stamp. To decrease processing time, USACC aligns with most Arab embassies in accepting shipping documents (C/O and C/I) in PDF format via email.

Upon receipt, the invoice or certificate of origin will be stamped and delivered to the required embassy. Refer to the table below to determine the price to certify C/O, C/I. or both from USACC and the embassy. You may choose to send the documents by mail, or by PDF if the embassy allows.
