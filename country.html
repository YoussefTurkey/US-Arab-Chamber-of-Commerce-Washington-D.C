Algerian Embassy Legalization

The US Arab Chamber of Commerce assists U.S. exporters and corporations to expedite the legalization process and obtain the required authentications in a timely manner.

Once we receive your document we will do the following:
  1. Stamp it by the US Arab Chamber of Commerce
  2. Authenticate it from US Department of State
  3. Legalize it by the Embassy of Algeria in Washington DC
Legalization Of Business Documents
(all type of documents except invoice or certificate of origin)

The US Arab Chamber of Commerce legalizes business and federally issued documents from the Embassy of Algeria in Washington D.C.

Service Type Fees Estimated busniness time
Service Fee $60.00 3 busniess days
US Department of State Fee $20.00 3 busniess days

Business documents must be signed, notarized, and certified from the secretary of the state such as:
  • Affidavit
  • Power of Attorney- POA
  • Assignments
  • Business Letters
  • Certificate of Status
  • Agency Agreements
  • Letter of Authorization
  • Articles of Organization
  • Articles of Incorporation
  • Certificate of Good Standing
  • Certificate of Merger
  • Certificate of Registration
  • Company Bylaws
  • Certificate of Amendments
  • Certificate of Incumbency
  • Board Resolution
  • Certificate of Existence
  • Certificate of Formation
  • Certificate of Formation
  • Certificate of Analysis
  • ISO Certificate
  • Ingredient Certificate
Federally Issued documents do not require state certification. Must be original, such as:
  • Certificate of free Sale-CFS
  • Certificate to Foreign Government-CFG
  • Certificate of Exportability
  • Certificate of Pharmaceutical Products-COPP
  • Patent Certificate
  • Trademark Certificate
  • Phytosanitary Certificate
  • FBI Background Check

Legalization For Commercial Documents (Commercial Invoice-Certificate Of Origin)

Certificate of Origin and Commercial Invoice destined for use in Algeria must be:
  • Original document signed by a company official and notarized
  • Certified from the same secretary of the state of the notary
Once we receive your document we will do the following:
  1. Stamp it by the US Arab Chamber of Commerce
  2. Authenticate it from US Department of State
  3. Legalize it by the Embassy of Algeria in Washington DC
Fees One document (C/I or C/O)
Service Fee $75.00
US Department of State Fee $20.00
Embassy Fee of Algeria $75.00
US Arab Chamber of Commerce Stamp fee $35.00
The total fees $205.00
Estimated Processing Time 33 business days
Expected completion date is Wednesday, April 20, 2022
